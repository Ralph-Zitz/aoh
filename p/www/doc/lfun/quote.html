SYNOPSIS

mixed quote(mixed)

DESCRIPTION

Converts arrays to quoted arrays and strings to symbols. Symbols and quoted arrays get quoted once more.

EXAMPLE

	quote("foo") -> 'foo
	quote(({1,2,3})) -> '({1,2,3})

HISTORY

Introduced in 3.2@70
