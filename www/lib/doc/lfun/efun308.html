SYNOPSIS

void efun308(object item, object env)

DESCRIPTION

The item is moved into its new environment env, which may be 0. This efun is to be used in the move_object() hook, as it does nothing else than moving the item - no calls to init() or such.

Don't use it in your own objects!

HISTORY

Introduced in 3.2.1@1

Just in case somebody wonders what kind of names efun308 is it isn't an efun name at all, but rather a makeshift because there is none yet; hopefully, with the existance of the efun (and maybe others), the issue of their naming will become a bit more interesting than what command shell was used on a room-filling dinosaur of a computer some decades ago.

Hyp proposes to christen efun308() 'set_environment()'.
